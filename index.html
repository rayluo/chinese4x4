A fun, interactive way to learn Chinese.

Hello

Welcome to Chinese Assimilation Project, made by a group of passionate high schoolers that want to make learning Chinese idioms, words, and poems are fun and efficient. The Chinese language has thousands of years of history, so of course, learning all the pieces of language that has accumlated is difficult. However, we think that internalizing all the bits and pieces of this culture is valuble. It's a gratifying experience that will make one more open-minded and will help you better understand the values and traditions of over a billion people on this planet. It's also something can simply show off to your friends.
By implementing a spaced repetition and the 4x4 technique, we crafted our interactive tools to be as fun and efficient as possible.
